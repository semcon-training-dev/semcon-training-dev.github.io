Help and support

Whenever you encounter a problem with your computer, mobile or IP telephony, or just need some IT related information - MySupport is the way to go. First, look at the MySupport Portal where you will find information about the ongoing disturbances, answer on the most asked questions and information about all IT services. The easiest way to reach MySupport Portal is via the shortcut on your desktop. MySupport exists in 12 languages e.g. English, Dutch, German and French. Your task here is to go into the site and get an overview of the help available.
