Volvo Trucks

The Volvo brand enjoys a solid position worldwide. It is one of the world’s best known and respected brands. Volvo is a premium truck brand that stands for innovation, and the typical Volvo Truck customer is someone who wants technically advanced trucks, who focus on both revenue and operating costs, and that values brands with excellent reputation.

Eicher motors

Eicher is one of the largest players in the Indian mainstream commercial vehicle market, with a strong image of fuel efficiency and profitability for the customers. Eicher is leading the modernization of the commercial vehicle industry in India and in the emerging world.

Mack

Mack is one of the strongest heavy- duty truck brands in the North American market. Mack is very big in the heavy duty construction truck segment and is renowned for its outstanding reliability, durability and strength. The typical Mack customer is someone who requires tough trucks for demanding applications, supported by a family experience.

Nova bus

is a leading North American provider of sustainable transit solutions, including buses, high-capacity vehicles and integrated intelligent transportation systems.

Prevost

With more than 10,000 vehicles on North American roads, Prevost is a leading North American manufacturer of premium touring coaches and bus shells for high-end motorhomes and specialty conversions.

Renault Trucks

Since 1894, building on the legacy of French truck industry know-how, Renault Trucks supplies transport professionals with robust tools and robust relationships. The typical Renault Trucks customer is an entrepreneur who is focused on operational cost and requires tools and support optimized for their applications.

Volvo bus

Volvo is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of heavy buses, and also delivers chassis, transport solutions and telematics.

SDLG

is a leading brand in the Chinese construction machinery industry, especially for wheel loaders. The SDLG brand is sold primarily in China and other emerging markets and is now being introduced to selected mature markets, e.g. Brazil and North America.

Dongfeng Trucks

Dongfeng Trucks is one of China´s leading truck brand and it was established in 1969. It is originated from the Chinese culture and has further been developed through craftsmanship with trust, professionalism and global techniques. The company is now expanding globally and is gradually establishing its global sales & service networks. 

Eicher motors

Eicher is one of the largest players in the Indian mainstream commercial vehicle market, with a strong image of fuel efficiency and profitability for the customers. Eicher is leading the modernization of the commercial vehicle industry in India and in the emerging world.

Terex

comes from a strong hauling heritage and produces robust, reliable articulated and rigid dump trucks for the most extreme of hauling applications.

UD Buses

UD buses is a bus range developed specifically for growth markets. The bus range includes both city buses and coaches. UD buses will offer passengers a modern transport solution while at the same time focus on the operational essentials: fuel efficiency, reliability and optimized uptime.

Volvo Penta

Volvo Penta is a world-leading supplier of engines and drive systems for leisure and professional boats, as well as for industrial applications such as generator sets and engines for materials handling. Volvo Penta engines are known for their innovative design and high-performance characteristics.

UD Trucks

UD Trucks markets medium-duty and heavy-duty trucks in Japan, Asia, Australia, Africa, Middle East and Latin America. The typical UD truck customer is cost-driven and network-oriented, seeking value by balancing cost with performance and dependability. UD Trucks’ Brand promise is “Going the Extra Mile”.

Volvo Construction Equipment

Volvo is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of construction equipment, such as articulated haulers, wheel loaders, excavators, compactors and pavers.
