The Volvo Group Organisation


GROUP TRUCKS:

We are organized into Group Trucks Technology, called GTT. Here we develop and design our products and technology. Group Trucks Operations, called GTO, is where we build our trucks and handle logistics.



BUSINESS AREAS:

Volvo Construction Equipment - one of the world’s leading construction equipment businesses, and we sell our products, parts and services in more than 150 countries.

Volvo Penta - our company that makes, builds and sells world-leading engines for boats as well as industrial engines.



THERE IS ALSO:

Volvo Buses, the world’s second largest bus manufacturer, Governmental Sales, which focuses on meeting governmental needs, and Volvo Financial Services, who support our customers with the finance side of things.
